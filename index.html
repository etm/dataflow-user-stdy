Welcome!

Participate in this questionnaire about BPMN dataflow modelling and visualization. Please read the consent statements below and proceed for a series of questions and tasks.

Who Can Participate?

If you are aged 18 or older you are eligible to participate.

What is the purpose of this study?

This survey is part of a research project on BPMN data flow visualization. All process models are real-world executable process models, created by domain experts and refined to be as usable as possible.

What will I do?

This survey will ask general questions about your programming experience and modeling skills. It also includes questions that evaluate your level of understanding of different BPMN dataflow, and how its is connected to tasks and gateways. In addition, you will be asked to identify strengths and weaknesses of different approaches.

Procedure

This survey is completely voluntary. There are no negative consequences if you don’t want to take it. If you start the survey, you can always change your mind and stop at any time. Your participation in this online survey is completely anonymous. No information you share electronically can be traced to you or the computer you use. Your participation in the survey indicates you read this consent information and agreed to participate in this anonymous survey. Depending upon the depth of your responses, the survey will range from 15 to 25 minutes.
